Support

Need help with Happy Chabby? Find answers to common questions below or contact us directly.

Getting Started

How do I set up my hydration goal?

When you first open Happy Chabby, you'll go through a quick onboarding where you enter your weight and activity level. The app will calculate a personalized daily hydration goal for you. You can always adjust this in Settings.

How do I log a drink?

From the main screen, tap the quick-add buttons (+100ml or +250ml) or tap "Custom" to enter a specific amount. You can also select different beverage types like water, coffee, tea, or juice.

Who is Chabby?

Chabby is your adorable capybara companion! He reacts to your hydration progress throughout the day. The more water you drink, the happier Chabby becomes. Keep him hydrated!

Features

How do streaks work?

You earn a streak day when you reach 100% of your daily hydration goal. Keep reaching your goal every day to build your streak! If you miss a day, your streak resets to zero.

How do I earn achievements?

Achievements are unlocked automatically as you use the app. You can earn badges for streak milestones, total water consumed, trying different beverages, and more. Check the Achievements tab to see all available badges.

What are coins used for?

Coins are earned by unlocking achievements. They can be used for premium features within the app. Keep hydrating and earning achievements to collect more coins!

How do I set up reminders?

Go to Settings and enable "Reminders." You can customize how often you want to be reminded to drink water. The default interval is every 60 minutes.

Troubleshooting

My data isn't syncing

Happy Chabby stores all your data locally on your device for privacy. Data does not sync between devices. If you switch phones, you'll start fresh with a new profile.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings and scroll down to find "Clear All Data." This will permanently delete all your drink logs, achievements, and settings. This action cannot be undone.

The app is running slowly

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed from the App Store or Google Play.

Subscriptions & Purchases

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store:

  • iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Android: Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel, go to your device's subscription settings (see above). Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

I was charged but don't have premium features

Try restoring your purchases: go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." If the issue persists, contact us with your purchase receipt.

Request a Refund

Refunds are processed by Apple or Google, not directly by us. To request a refund:

  • iOS: Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Android: Visit Google Play Help
